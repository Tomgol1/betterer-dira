How calculations work

Click to expand

Preferred populations: ♿ Wheelchair/Disabled, 🎖️ Combat Fighters, 🛡️ Active Reserve, 🏠 Locals.

  1. Allocate designated houses to each preferred group: used = min(designated, applicants).
  2. Combat exception: Unclaimed Combat houses go to Active Reserve first; if still unclaimed, they go to the general pool.
  3. General pool houses = unclaimed ♿ + unclaimed (🛡️ after combat spillover) + unclaimed 🏠 + all Regular houses (Total - all designated).
  4. General pool competitors = Regular applicants + overflow from all preferred groups (those who didn’t get a designated house).
  5. Lottery probability = min(1, GeneralPoolHouses / GeneralPoolCompetitors).
  6. City probability (win at least one in that city) = 1 − Π(1 − plottery).
  7. Overall probability across your selected cities (max 3) = 1 − Π(1 − pcity).

All totals are inclusive of preferred populations. Regular applicants are: Applicants − (Wheelchair_Applicants + Combat_Applicants + Reserve_Applicants + Local_Applicants).
